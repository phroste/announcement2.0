Legion returns for Season 2 in 2018

Legion is FX and Marvel Television's new series exploring the universe of the X-Men character Legion, created by comic book veterans Chris Claremont and Bill Sienkiewicz. Series showrunner, director, and writer Noah Hawley (FX's Fargo) brings a unique perspective to the storied world of Marvel Television and adds his chapter to the Marvel universe. Unravel the mysteries of the Legion universe. Only on FX.
